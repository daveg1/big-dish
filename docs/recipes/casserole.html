    Casserole

    ← Back to Recipes

    Food Info

    • Nuts free, gluten free, vegan friendly

    Cooking Time: 50 min

    Ingredients

    • 1 tbsp olive
    • 1 onion
    • 3 garlic cloves
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • ½ tsp cumin
    • 1 tbsp dried thyme
    • 3 carrots
    • 2 celery sticks
    • 1 red pepper
    • 1 yellow pepper
    • 2 x 400g cans tomatoes
    • 1 vegetable stock cube
    • 2 courgettes
    • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
    • 250g lentils

    Method

    1. Heat 1 tbsp olive in a large pan. Add 1 chopped onion and cook gently for 5 – 10 mins until softened.

    2. Add 3 sliced garlic cloves, 1 tsp paprika, ½ tsp cumin, 1 tbsp dried thyme, 3 sliced carrots, 2 sliced celery sticks, 1 chopped red pepper and 1 chopped yellow pepper and cook for 5 minutes.

    3. Add two 400g cans tomatoes, 250ml vegetable stock, 2 sliced courgettes and 2 sprigs fresh thyme and cook for 20 - 25 minutes.

    4. Take out the thyme sprigs. Stir in 250g cooked lentils and bring back to a simmer. Serve with basmati rice.
