    What Is Big Dish?

    Big Dish is a brand-new cooking society based right here at RGU. We often hold events, gatherings, workships and the like with our members, although newcomers are always welcome! Interested and want to know more? We implore you to read our about page and if you so feel inclined, join us at our next meeting—you may be our newest member!
